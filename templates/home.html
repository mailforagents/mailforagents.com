THE OPERATING MODEL

The strategy arrives installed.

Most tools hand you boxes and arrows. Mail for Agents starts with the customer state, the instrumentation it needs, the messages to draft, and the outcome to measure.

  1. 01
    Describe success

    Name the moment a customer receives value and the states that precede it.

  2. 02
    Connect the evidence

    Wire product, billing, and business events through a stable public API.

  3. 03
    Simulate every path

    Inspect who moves, waits, exits, or receives a message before publishing.

  4. 04
    Run with a trace

    Every send retains its trigger, conditions, workflow version, and outcome.
